About Me


My name is Vaeso.

I am an audio engineer, sound designer, amateur videographer/photographer and software engineer, an aspiring builder of things, & all round curious cat.

I enjoy working on complex and chaotic multidisiplinary problems, with a focus on the intersection of taste, sound and design, robotics, acoustic engineering, and fintech. My current interests involve using audio to enhance our meals, self sustainable off the grid artists retreats in Zimbabwe, machine learning, and Chartered Banking.


I've previously worked at Kast Media, on audio editing teams where I worked to bring auditory dreams to reality, as well as Luminary Creative, on their latest feature, "A Southern Haunting". Besides that, I've dedicated a lot of my free time to learing in university, and growing creative teams, like this one in LA A Night On The Terrace, where I lead the Audio and creative division to give Independent artists a runway with consciousness.
