About Me


My name is Vaeso.

I am an audio engineer, sound designer, amateur videographer/photographer and software engineer, an aspiring builder of things, & all round curious cat.

I enjoy working on complex and chaotic multidisiplinary problems, with a focus on the intersection of taste, sound & design.


I've worked at Kast Media, as a sound editor for podcasts, Luminary Creative, on their latest feature, "A Southern Haunting" as a Sound Designer. Currently conducting research on Odor & Audio (Join Research Team), Proud Owner & Host of A Night On The Terrace, a runway/moat for Indie artists during Covid-19 .
