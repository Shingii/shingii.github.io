About Me


My name is Vaeso.

I am an audio engineer, sound designer, amateur videographer/photographer and software engineer, an aspiring builder of things, & all round curious cat.

I enjoy working on complex and chaotic multidisiplinary problems, with a focus on the intersection of taste, sound, design, robotics, acoustical engineering, and fintech. My current interests involve using audio to enhance our meals, self sustainable/off-grid creative retreats, machine learning,banking.


I've worked at Kast Media, as a sound editor for podcasts, Luminary Creative, on their latest feature, "A Southern Haunting" as a Sound Designer. Proud Owner & Host of A Night On The Terrace, a runway/moat for Indie artists during Covid-19 .
